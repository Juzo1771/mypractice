Scrapper

He was trained to perform circus acts for ghoul customers, and earned "Good Boy Points" — Big Madam's deceitful system she used to torture him. Eventually, he began working in the Ghoul Restaurant as a Scrapper named "Juuzou."

Special Class Investigator

Juuzou Suzuya was promoted to Special Class investigator, at only 22 years old, making him one of the strongest CCG members alive. Keijin Nakarai was promoted to First Class Investigator, while all other members were promoted to Rank 1 Investigators.

Dragon General
